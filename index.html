What is Shirataki Noodle?

Shirataki Noodle is a low-calorie food made from glucomannan, a type of fiber found in the Asian konjac plant.

Here are some of the benefits gained by eating Shirataki Noodle:

1. High in Viscous Fiber

2. Can Help You Lose Weight

3. Can Reduce Blood Sugar and Insulin Levels

4. May Lower Cholesterol

5. May Relieve Constipation
